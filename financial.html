Key Government Schemes for Financial Empowerment

Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

APY is a social security scheme focused on the unorganized sector. It guarantees a minimum monthly pension to the subscriber after the age of $60$, ranging from $₹1,000$ to $₹5,000$, depending on the contribution.

  • Guaranteed minimum pension post-60.
  • Government co-contribution for eligible subscribers.
  • Focus on retirement savings for low-income workers.

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)

PMJDY is a national mission for financial inclusion to ensure access to financial services, namely, Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account (BSBDA), remittance, credit, insurance, and pension in an affordable manner.

  • Zero balance bank accounts.
  • Rupay Debit Card and accidental insurance cover.
  • Overdraft facility available after $6$ months of satisfactory operation.
