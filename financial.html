Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)

PMJDY is a national mission for **financial inclusion** to ensure access to essential financial services like a Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account (BSBDA), remittance, credit, and more.

  • **Zero balance** bank accounts.
  • Rupay Debit Card and accidental insurance cover.
  • Overdraft facility available after 6 months of satisfactory operation.

SSY is a **small savings scheme** targeted at the parents of a girl child. It encourages parents to build a fund for the future education and marriage expenses of their daughter.
