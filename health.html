Digital Access to National and State Health Schemes

ABHA Card

The **Ayushman Bharat Health Account (ABHA)** is a unique **14-digit health ID** that securely links your digital health records across various healthcare providers, ensuring continuity of care.

Create/Access ABHA ID

Mahatma Jotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana

This Maharashtra state scheme provides **cashless treatment** for specified diseases in empaneled hospitals. It covers a wide range of surgeries and therapies for eligible families.

Check Eligibility & Details

COWIN Portal

The official digital platform for **public health immunization programs**. Use this portal to register, book vaccination appointments, and download your final digital vaccination certificates.
